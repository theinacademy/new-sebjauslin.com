The Intervention

Over six months, Seb worked with her on visibility, confidence and the ability to translate her expertise into commercial language that senior leadership could hear.

The ROI

Replacing a Global Director typically costs 150–200% of annual salary. The coaching investment was a fraction of that; the return was a retained, promoted CFO.

The Outcome

Leading Through a Crisis — The Estée Lauder Companies

The Situation

The Estée Lauder Companies were in the middle of a significant restructure. Redundancies had been made, uncertainty was everywhere, and a 30-person leadership team needed more than a workshop.

The Intervention

Seb designed and facilitated a full-day Leading Through Change workshop using the BE > DO > HAVE Architecture™.

The ROI

Each specialist departure can cost an estimated €30,000–€80,000. Retaining a full team of 30 through disruption represents a return many times the workshop investment.

The Outcome

  • Team performance returned to pre-restructure levels
